Catering FAQs

What is your minimum guest count?

Our minimum catering is for 20 guests. For smaller groups, please contact us directly at +234 815 509 5977 to discuss options.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance to ensure availability and allow time for menu planning. Rush bookings within 72 hours may have limited options.

Do you provide service staff?

Yes! All our catering packages include professional service staff for setup, food service, and cleanup. We handle everything so you can enjoy your event.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can prepare vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary-specific meals. Let us know your requirements when booking.

Do you provide setup and cleanup?

Yes, complete setup and breakdown is included in all our packages. We also provide tables, serving dishes, and disposables as needed.

What is your payment policy?

A 50% deposit is required to confirm booking, with the balance due 3 days before the event. We accept bank transfers and cash.
